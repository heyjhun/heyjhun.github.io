I thought it is getting better.

I thought I am seeing the light.

It was just a glimpse. I saw a hole, a tiny one.

My hands won't even fit. The light is just peeking from the dime-sized hole.

It just gave me a sense of what was in there.

It's gone now, I did not even notice.

Slowly getting out of reach. Slowly fading away.

It was just a tiny light, but it made me feel like it was the whole of it.

It is getting dark again, the longer I look, the dimmer it gets.

It is dark again. I don't know how long will it be.

How long till I get to the light, the real light?
