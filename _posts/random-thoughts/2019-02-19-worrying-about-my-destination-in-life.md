Random thoughts while I am on a comfy ride.

Life is more than just trying to get to the destination.

Yes. cliche as it gets, but I am still contemplating of it until now.

I try to figure out what my life could look like 5 years from now, 10 years from now. I want to have this kind of life in the future, my dreams, a beautiful house, a trendy car, a big business, a happy family. 

When I get all of these, is it the end of my destination? 

I may get lost even if I have a good plan. I may take different path other than what I have planned. Life can fuck me up making my journey farther away to my destination. Running around, going to an alternate road, even alleys. 

Along the way, I will face a lot of uncertainties. Struggles. Ups and downs. It prepares me to get to my destination. 

Who knows where is my real destination, that alone is something I am not sure of. There is an ultimate destination for all of us, it could be somewhere we really wanted to be since the beginning, or we will end up into something least we expected. Worst case is, can I even finish this "amazing race?"

One thing that is a bit clear to me now, destination could be uncertain even if it is what you wanted and planned, but what I can control is my own phasing. I can just walk or run or ride a vehicle.  

So why I worry about my future? My temporary destination? 

I need to move from x to y. 
I need to keep moving so that I could get a better chance to get closer to the destination. Move!
