"Leave, we are broken people."

This line written as a note in a piece of tissue lying on my table one morning when I arrived in the office.

I do not know who wrote it. There is no name written along with the line. 

I was thinking maybe it was not for me. Maybe someone misplaced it. Maybe someone is playing with me. 

The moment I saw it, I read it like multiple times. There were some flashes of memories from the time I started working in this company. I was thinking of all the possible things that made them feel this way. I doubted myself, I doubted my being, I doubted the goodness in me. Did I do something wrong for me to be blamed by someone saying that I broke everyone. It was kind of off and inconsiderate for that someone to tell me those words. However, I was thinking as well that maybe there was an instance I did something wrong, but I don't know what yet. 

I was still not ok with this note. This is like the first time I was asked to leave blaming me for I have "broken" them.

Maybe sooner or later, I would feel ok with this. Right now, I am just thinking that maybe this is the kind of push I need to get to that concrete decision of finally leaving. I was still holding back before I got this note, but I guess this is the "not so hard way" of telling me that I have to move on, and be somewhere else.
