"Leave, we are broken people."

This line written as a note in a piece of tissue lying on my table one morning when I arrived in the office.

I do not know who wrote it. There is no name written along with the line. 

I was thinking maybe it was not for me. Maybe someone misplaced it. Maybe someone is playing with me. 

The moment I saw it, I read it like multiple times. There were some flashes of memories from the time I started working in this company. I doubted myself, I doubted my being, I doubted the goodness in me. Did I do something wrong for me to be blamed by someone saying that I broke everyone. It was hurtful and inconsiderate for that someone to tell me those words.
