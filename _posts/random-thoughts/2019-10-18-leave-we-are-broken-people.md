“Leave, we are broken people.”

A friend of mine told me a story about this.

He said that this line was written on a piece of tissue lying on his table one morning when he arrived in their office. He took a photo of it and showed to me. Natawa pa sya ng bahagya kasi hindi tama yong spelling ng broken (spelled as "brocken").

![leave-we-are-broken-people-line-written-on-a-tissue](/assets/img/leave-we-are-broken-people-post-image.jpg){:class="img-responsive" alt="leave-we-are-broken-people-line-written-on-a-tissue"}

He does not know who wrote it. There is no name written along with the line.

He was thinking maybe it was not meant for him. Maybe someone misplaced it. Maybe someone is playing with him.

The moment he saw it, he said that he read it like multiple times. He was a bit sad as I can see in his eyes. I know that this guy is a very nice person and he can sometimes be a people pleaser because he wanted to work with everyone in a harmonious way. I know that he always adapts to others well enough.

He added that while thinking of it, there were some flashes of his memories from the time he started working in that company. He was thinking of all the possible things that made them feel this way. He added that he doubted himself, he doubted his being, he doubted the goodness in him. He asked himself “Did I do something wrong for me to be blamed by someone saying that I broke every one.”

I cannot blame him when he said that the person who wrote it is somehow inconsiderate to tell him those words. However, he was thinking as well that maybe there was an instance he did something wrong, but he doesn’t know what could it be.

He was still not ok when he was telling me this. I can see that he was a bit upset. This is like the first time he shared something a bit serious to me. If it was me, I may feel the same way. 

I think that it is not easy to be asked to leave in a place where you learned to enjoy the company of the people. He is telling me that he like those people, they are fun to be with. However, he cannot tell me a name who can leave him that note. Maybe he is playing nice not to blame or point a finger on someone. 

At least, he is not angry or mad at someone, he is just upset with the situation.

Maybe sooner or later, he would feel ok with this. Right now, he is just thinking that maybe this is the kind of push he needs to get to that concrete decision of finally leaving. He was still holding back before he got this note, but I think it is better for him. I guess this is the “not so hard way” of telling him that he has to move on and be somewhere else. Maybe that somewhere else is the place where he mentioned he wanted to go.

One realization that I got from this is that no matter how much effort you put in making sure that you are one hundred percent in good terms with all your colleagues and boss, there will always be an instance that it won’t.

Good luck to you my friend. You can do it. It is not like the end of the world. There is a lot more out there to explore there.
